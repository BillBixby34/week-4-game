Orb Absconding

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four orbs below. By clicking on a orb you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Each orb holds a different value!

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, the values of each orb will change.
